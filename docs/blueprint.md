# **App Name**: Krishi Mitra

## Core Features:

- Login/Signup: Login/signup page with authentication and authorization (name, email, password, phone, address), language select for display and voice. Login once.
- Image Analysis: Capture images of crops/animals/plants and use a pre-trained ML model to identify quality defects, diseases, or decay.
- Sorting and Grading: Recommend how to sort harvests based on quality (premium, market-ready, discard/process) using a reasoning tool.
- Storage Suggestions: Provide real-time, simple storage instructions based on identified quality and weather conditions, accessed using the user's GPS location.
- Voice Input: Allow farmers to describe visible conditions via voice to receive recommendations, as an alternative to image capture.
- Local Market Connection: Enable farmers to list upcoming harvests and connect with local businesses; provide market information based on quality grades.
- PestPatrol Community Outbreak Map: Anonymously geo-tag incidents and feed data into a live outbreak map. Proactively alerts farmers within 10-15 km of a threat with an app notification.
- Intelligent Dashboard: Location-based advisories on irrigation, spray timings, and planting/harvesting windows by connecting with public weather APIs and integrating PestPatrol data.
- Multi-language support: The user selects at first to display the app in english, hindi or odia languages

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant green (#A2CD5A) to evoke growth and freshness.
- Background color: Light, desaturated green (#E2F0D9) to create a calm and natural feel.
- Accent color: Yellow-gold (#D4AC0D) for highlighting important information.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a modern and friendly sans-serif, suitable for both headlines and body text. It balances a contemporary look with a touch of warmth, making the app approachable for farmers.
- Use clear and intuitive icons related to farming and agriculture.
- A clean and user-friendly layout, ensuring easy navigation for users of all digital literacy levels.
- Use subtle animations and transitions to create a visually appealing and engaging experience.
